## 1. LLM Platforms Comparison

### Overview Table

| Tool | Best For | Marketing Strength | Free Trial | Pricing (Jan 2026) |
|------|----------|-------------------|------------|-------------------|
| **ChatGPT** (OpenAI) | General copywriting, brainstorming, rapid iteration | Fastest response, creative variation generation, plugin ecosystem | ✅ Free tier | Free / $20/mo Plus |
| **Claude** (Anthropic) | Long-form content, brand voice consistency, nuanced understanding | Superior context retention, safer content handling, excellent for legal/sensitive topics | ✅ Free tier | Free / $20/mo Pro |
| **Gemini** (Google) | Multimodal content, data analysis, workspace integration | Native Google integration, handles images+text simultaneously | ✅ Free tier | Free (or via Workspace) |
| **Perplexity** | Research, competitive analysis, fact-checking | Real-time web search, automatic citations, current information | ✅ Free tier | Free / $20/mo Pro |

### Use Case Mapping

**Campaign Ideation (Brainstorming 20+ concepts quickly)**
- **Best:** ChatGPT
- **Why:** Fastest iteration speed, excels at divergent creative thinking
- **Guerilla Example:** Generate 30 disruptive billboard concepts for Toyota in 5 minutes

**Strategy Document Writing (10-page brand strategy)**
- **Best:** Claude
- **Why:** Maintains consistent tone/voice across long documents, superior reasoning
- **Guerilla Example:** Write comprehensive campaign strategy document for Anglo NuGen tech launch

**Client Research (Understanding a new industry/competitor)**
- **Best:** Perplexity
- **Why:** Real-time web access, provides sources for fact-checking
- **Guerilla Example:** Research AR usage in automotive advertising across South Africa

**Pitch Deck Creation (Combining text, data, visuals)**
- **Best:** Gemini
- **Why:** Multimodal capabilities, Google Slides/Docs integration
- **Guerilla Example:** Create DStv pitch deck with market data visualizations

**Social Media Posts (Quick, on-brand content)**
- **Best:** ChatGPT or Copy.ai
- **Why:** Speed + brand voice adaptability
- **Guerilla Example:** Generate 15 Instagram caption variations for client approval

**Brand Voice Analysis (Understanding client tone)**
- **Best:** Claude
- **Why:** Pattern recognition in writing style, nuanced understanding
- **Guerilla Example:** Analyze Guerilla's "anti-traditional" voice and replicate it

### Strengths & Weaknesses

#### ChatGPT
**Strengths:**
- Lightning-fast responses
- Excellent creative brainstorming
- Strong plugin ecosystem (web browsing, image generation via DALL-E)
- Most familiar interface for beginners

**Weaknesses:**
- Can be overly verbose without proper prompting
- Sometimes too "safe" or generic in tone
- Knowledge cutoff (not real-time without plugins)

**Best for Guerilla:** Creative concepting, rapid copywriting iterations

---

#### Claude
**Strengths:**
- Exceptional long-form writing quality
- Superior at maintaining brand voice consistency
- Handles complex, nuanced instructions better
- More "thoughtful" responses

**Weaknesses:**
- Slower response times than ChatGPT
- More conservative content policies (can be too cautious)
- Less playful for brainstorming

**Best for Guerilla:** Client strategy docs, maintaining brand guidelines across deliverables

---

#### Gemini
**Strengths:**
- Native multimodal (text + images simultaneously)
- Google Workspace integration (Docs, Sheets, Slides)
- Can analyze uploaded images/documents
- Strong data analysis capabilities

**Weaknesses:**
- Less creative than ChatGPT or Claude for pure copywriting
- Interface less refined than competitors
- Smaller community/fewer resources

**Best for Guerilla:** Pitch presentations, analyzing client briefs with attached images, data-heavy content

---

#### Perplexity
**Strengths:**
- Real-time web search (always current)
- Automatic source citations
- Excellent for fact-checking and research
- Clean, focused interface

**Weaknesses:**
- Not ideal for creative writing
- Limited customization/personalization
- Smaller context window than competitors

**Best for Guerilla:** Competitive research, trend analysis, fact-checking for client presentations

## 2. Copy & Content Tools Comparison

### Overview Table

| Tool | Best Use Case | Guerilla Application | Free Trial | Pricing |
|------|---------------|---------------------|------------|---------|
| **Jasper** | Brand voice memory, templates, team collaboration | Maintaining Toyota/DStv brand guidelines across campaigns | ✅ 7-day trial | $49/mo Creator / $125/mo Teams |
| **Copy.ai** | Ad variations at scale, A/B testing, rapid generation | Generating 50+ billboard headline variants for testing | ✅ Free tier (2000 words/mo) | Free / $49/mo Pro |
| **Writesonic** | SEO content, blog posts, landing pages | Creating blog content for client websites | ✅ Free tier (10k words/mo) | Free / $19/mo Pro |

### Detailed Comparison

#### When to Use Jasper
**Scenario:** You manage 5 long-term clients (e.g., Toyota, DStv, Anglo) who each have strict brand guidelines.

**Value Proposition:**
- Save client brand voices as templates
- Team members can access same brand profiles
- Ensures consistency across deliverables from different team members

**Guerilla Use Case:**  
*"Toyota campaign needs 10 different assets (billboards, social, radio scripts). Three team members are working on it. Jasper's brand voice profile ensures all outputs sound cohesively 'Toyota.'"*

**Pricing Consideration:** Best for teams, justifies cost at $125/mo for 3+ users

---

#### When to Use Copy.ai
**Scenario:** You need massive variation for testing—50 ad headlines, 30 social posts, etc.

**Value Proposition:**
- Generates huge volumes quickly
- Built-in variation features
- Affordable for individual users

**Guerilla Use Case:**  
*"DStv Internet launch needs A/B testing for paid social. Generate 100 headline variations in 20 minutes, narrow to top 10 for testing."*

**Pricing Consideration:** Free tier sufficient for testing, upgrade for teams

---

#### When to Use Writesonic
**Scenario:** Client needs ongoing blog/SEO content.

**Value Proposition:**
- SEO optimization built-in
- Landing page copy
- Most affordable paid tier

**Guerilla Use Case:**  
*"Private Property client needs monthly property market blog posts optimized for South African search terms."*

**Pricing Consideration:** Most budget-friendly at $19/mo

## 3. SEO & Research Tools Comparison

### Overview Table

| Tool | Primary Function | When to Use | Trial | Pricing |
|------|-----------------|-------------|-------|---------|
| **Surfer SEO** | On-page optimization, content scoring | Optimizing web content for search rankings | ✅ 7-day | $89/mo Essential |
| **MarketMuse** | Content strategy, topic clustering | Planning comprehensive content calendars | ✅ 7-day | $149/mo Standard |
| **Frase** | Content briefs, outlining, Q&A research | Briefing freelance writers for client work | ✅ 5-day | $45/mo Basic |

### Use Case Decision Tree

**Question 1:** Do you already have content that needs optimization?
- **YES** → Use **Surfer SEO** (scores existing content, suggests improvements)
- **NO** → Continue to Question 2

**Question 2:** Do you need a comprehensive content strategy (30+ articles)?
- **YES** → Use **MarketMuse** (identifies topic clusters, gaps)
- **NO** → Continue to Question 3

**Question 3:** Do you need to brief writers to create content?
- **YES** → Use **Frase** (generates detailed outlines and briefs)

### Guerilla Application Examples

**Surfer SEO Example:**
- **Client:** DStv Internet
- **Task:** Optimize their "Best Internet Packages South Africa" landing page
- **Result:** Surfer scores page 45/100, recommends adding keywords "fibre packages," "unlimited data SA," increases to 82/100

**MarketMuse Example:**
- **Client:** Private Property
- **Task:** Plan 6-month content strategy for property investment blog
- **Result:** MarketMuse identifies 40 topic clusters, prioritizes by search volume + competitiveness

**Frase Example:**
- **Client:** Amazing Spaces
- **Task:** Brief freelance writer to create "Top 10 Home Renovation Trends 2026"
- **Result:** Frase generates detailed outline with questions people are asking, suggested headers, competitor analysis

## 4. Automation & Workflow Tools

### Overview Table

| Tool | Complexity Level | Best For | Trial | Pricing |
|------|-----------------|----------|-------|---------|
| **Zapier** | Beginner-friendly | Simple 2-app connections | ✅ Free tier (100 tasks/mo) | Free / $20/mo Starter |
| **Make.com** | Intermediate | Visual complex workflows | ✅ Free tier (1000 ops/mo) | Free / $9/mo Core |
| **Relevance AI** | Advanced | Custom AI agents for repetitive tasks | ✅ Trial available | Custom pricing |

### When to Use Which

#### Zapier
**Best for:** Simple automations like "When [trigger], do [action]"

**Guerilla Examples:**
- When client approves social post in Slack → Auto-post to Instagram
- When new lead fills contact form → Add to HubSpot + Notify team in Slack  
- When new project in Asana → Create Google Drive folder structure

**Recommendation:** Start here. Easiest learning curve.

---

#### Make.com (formerly Integromat)
**Best for:** Multi-step workflows with conditional logic

**Guerilla Examples:**
- Client approval workflow: Draft in ChatGPT → Send to Slack for approval → If approved, post to social + log in database → If rejected, notify designer for revisions
- Campaign reporting: Pull data from Google Ads + Meta Ads + GA4 → Combine in spreadsheet → Generate report → Email to client

**Recommendation:** Graduate to Make once Zapier feels limiting

---

#### Relevance AI
**Best for:** Building custom AI agents that perform complex, repetitive tasks

**Guerilla Examples:**
- Build an AI agent that: Monitors client mentions on social media → Analyzes sentiment → Drafts response options → Sends to team for approval
- Monthly reporting agent: Pulls all campaign data → Writes performance summary → Generates charts → Compiles into presentation

**Recommendation:** Advanced use case, requires more setup time

## Decision Framework Summary

### "Which tool for this task?" Quick Guide

**Need:** Quick copywriting for client
→ **ChatGPT** (fastest iteration)

**Need:** 50 ad headline variations for A/B testing  
→ **Copy.ai** (volume generation)

**Need:** Consistent brand voice across 10-person team  
→ **Jasper** (brand voice templates)

**Need:** Research on South African market trends  
→ **Perplexity** (real-time search + citations)

**Need:** 15-page strategy document with nuanced reasoning  
→ **Claude** (long-form excellence)

**Need:** Pitch deck with text + data + charts  
→ **Gemini** (multimodal + Google Workspace)

**Need:** Optimize existing web content for SEO  
→ **Surfer SEO** (content scoring)

**Need:** Plan 6-month content calendar  
→ **MarketMuse** (strategic planning)

**Need:** Automate simple task (e.g., Slack → Instagram)  
→ **Zapier** (easiest automation)

**Need:** Complex multi-step workflow  
→ **Make.com** (visual workflow builder)

## Cost-Effectiveness Analysis

### Free Tier Stack (Month 1)
**Tools:** ChatGPT Free + Claude Free + Perplexity Free + Gemini + Zapier Free

**Capabilities:** 80% of daily tasks covered

**Recommendation:** Good for individual marketers

**Cost:** ~$200/month per team (3-5 people)  
**Capabilities:** Full agency workflow  
**Recommendation:** When AI becomes core to operations
