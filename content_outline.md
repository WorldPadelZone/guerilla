# Guerilla AI Training Automation Content Outline

## Foundations (Months 1–2)
*   **Introduction to AI in the marketing workflow**
*   **Understanding AI capabilities, limits, and risks**
*   **Overview of AI tools used in modern marketing teams**
*   **Prompt fundamentals for marketers (thinking, not syntax)**

## AI Image Generation (Months 3–4)
*   **AI image generation for campaign concepts**
*   **Prompting for style, brand tone, and consistency**
*   **Image iteration and refinement workflows**
*   **Using AI images responsibly in client work**

## AI Video Generation (Months 5–6)
*   **AI-assisted video creation for ads and social media**
*   **Storyboarding with AI**
*   **Short-form video generation and editing workflows**
*   **Integrating AI video into campaign pipelines**

## AI Copy & Content Creation (Months 7–8)
*   **Using LLMs for marketing copy and ideation**
*   **Campaign messaging and variation at scale**
*   **Social posts, ads, and landing page drafts**
*   **Maintaining brand voice with AI**

## AI for Campaign Strategy & Planning (Months 9–10)
*   **AI-assisted campaign ideation**
*   **Creative concept generation and testing**
*   **Audience persona development with AI**
*   **Competitive and trend analysis using AI**

## AI for Productivity & Automation (Months 11–12)
*   **AI for internal agency workflows**
*   **Brief analysis and summarisation**
*   **Creative QA and refinement using AI**
*   **Ethical, legal, and future considerations in AI-driven marketing**

---

## Homework Philosophy
*   **Every session ends with a practical task**
*   **Tasks simulate real client or campaign scenarios**
*   **Focus on thinking, prompt quality, and output evaluation**
*   **Designed for quick review, not heavy grading**
